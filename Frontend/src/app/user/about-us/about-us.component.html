Overview

RepAlert is a comprehensive software solution designed to streamline the management of medical representatives, track their activities, and generate insightful reports for administrators.

Key Features

  • Administrator Dashboard: Manage users, track live locations, and generate reports.
  • Sales Management: Set targets, track performance, and manage sales data.
  • Visit Management: Plan, review, and manage visits for users.
  • Doctor Management: Search and view detailed doctor data and visit history.
  • Live Tracking: Share real-time location during visits.
